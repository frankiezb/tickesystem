Getting Started

Welcome to our ticketing system! Here’s a quick guide to help you get started:

  • Creating a Ticket: To create a new ticket, navigate to the "New Ticket" page and fill out the form with the required details. Click "Submit" to send your request.
  • Viewing Your Tickets: You can view all your submitted tickets by going to the "My Tickets" section. Here, you’ll find a list of all your tickets along with their statuses.
  • Updating a Ticket: To update an existing ticket, open the ticket from the "My Tickets" section, add your comments or attachments, and click "Update."
  • Resolving a Ticket: Once your issue has been addressed, you can mark the ticket as resolved. Simply open the ticket and click on the "Resolve" button.

FAQs

  • How do I reset my password?

    To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to receive a password reset link via email.

  • What should I do if I don't receive a response to my ticket?

    If you haven't received a response within the expected time frame, you can check the status of your ticket in the "My Tickets" section. If the issue persists, contact support directly for further assistance.

  • Can I re-open a resolved ticket?

    Yes, if you believe your issue hasn't been fully resolved, you can re-open a ticket by navigating to the resolved ticket and clicking on the "Re-open" button.

Troubleshooting

  • Issue with Ticket Submission: Ensure that all required fields are filled out correctly. If the problem persists, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser.
  • Attachments Not Uploading: Check the file size and format. Make sure it meets the system requirements. If you continue to experience issues, contact support for help.
  • Slow System Performance: Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the system is still slow, it may be undergoing maintenance. Check for any maintenance announcements or contact support.
